SPA

The phrase "sanus per aquam" (or "SPA") describes the use of water for health and wellness. We provide a range of SPA services and therapies at our restaurant that make use of water in some way to encourage healing and relaxation. We provide a variety of hydrotherapy services, including water massage and Jacuzzis, as well as balneotherapy services, including mineral baths and mud baths. Our qualified staff is here to help you with your selected treatments and to assure your comfort and security. We provide amenities including a swimming pool, hot tub, and steam room in addition to our SPA treatments for your enjoyment. We cordially invite you to visit our SPA to unwind and refresh.

Culinary training

For individuals wishing to advance their careers in the food sector and improve their culinary talents, our restaurant offers professional cook training courses. Our programs are meant to give students realworld, hands-on experience in a restaurant environment, allowing them to learn from seasoned chefs and put their knowledge to the test. We cover a variety of subjects in our classes, such as knife skills, food safety, culinary techniques, and menu planning. We offer a course that is ideal for you whether you are a novice or an experienced cook wishing to develop your abilities. We are dedicated to assisting our participants in achieving their goals by giving them the knowledge and abilities they need to thrive in the culinary industry

Special event

We are constantly searching for ways to improve the eating experience for our customers at our restaurant. We provide a range of special events all year long because of this. In addition to live music and other forms of entertainment, our restaurant frequently hosts wine evenings and cooking lessons. Our special events are a wonderful way to experience something new, learn from accomplished chefs and mixologists, and share a memorable meal with friends and loved ones. To give our guests a unique experience, we meticulously curate each event.
