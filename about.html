CEO

Welcome to THE ASSEMBL RESTAURANT, where we are committed to providing our guests with an exceptional dining experience. As the CEO of THE ASSEMBL RESTAURANT, I am proud to lead a team of talented individuals who share my passion for varieties cuisine and strive to exceed our guests' expectations every day. I have been in the THE ASSEMBL RESTAURANT industry for over 8 years, starting as a kitchen helper and working my way up through the ranks. I have a strong belief in using high-quality ingredients, supporting local suppliers, and creating a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere for our guests. As CEO, my role is to ensure that THE ASSEMBL RESTAURANT is a thriving and successful business, and to that end, I am constantly looking for ways to innovate and improve. Whether through our commitment to sustainability, our dedication to community involvement, or our focus on culinary excellence, I am proud of the values that guide THE ASSEMBL RESTAURANT and the impact we have on our guests and the community. HEAD CHEF

Meet our head chef, Aashi. With over more than 4 years of experience in the culinary industry, Chef Aashi brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to the kitchen.
